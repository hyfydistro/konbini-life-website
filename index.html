Synopsis

After flunking out of University, Natsu has been working the night shift at the local MegaKonbiniMart to make ends meet. She's got an annoying co-worker who calls her "Flunkee-chan" whose sole purpose in life is to make her miserable. There's the creepy businessman who's only interests are of the 2D persuasion, and he can't help scaring away other customers. The foreigner who buys snacks in bulk and is hiding a secret. And then there's signs of a customer with sticky fingers.

Characters

Hiroto Sagawa

"Grouchy"

Co-Worker

Grouchy has been on your back since day one. His attitude wswings from an A-Grade employee to a temperamental toddler when you are around. Your lack of enthusiasm towards work tends to be his trigger.

He's younger than you are but has been at MegaKonbiniMart longer so he is your senpai.

For him, the Konbini is his life.

Otaku Businessman

"Glasses"

Customer

Glasses has been hanging around the Konbini for awhile now, he can always be found reading in the magazine aisle. He's particularly enamored by a Magic Girl manga series to which he can talk about at great length.

He's a little awkward, and can come across as creepy but you know he is just a harmless otaku.

You don't know much else about him.

Foreigner

"Prince"

Customer

Prince has been stopping by the Konbini every night since he arrived in Japan. He enjoys the simple things and has found he has a particular fondness for Instant Ramen. He is a little ignorant at times but is well meaning.

He says he's a University student taking a gap year, but you are finding that hard to believe.
